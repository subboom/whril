Business Development Representative

Toronto, ON

Hey it's Chris and I wanted to welcome you to the fien team!! If you like discovering fun ideas, sharing interesting topics with others, and building impactful products within a small team then read on!

Our Product

At fien we want to help inspire real connections between content creators and audiences. Our mid-form video platform helps spark ideas, but we're really looking to hire an ambitious sales representative that will help develop our local reps partner program. If you care about working on products that help shape global communication – then you should consider having coffee with our team.

We are expecting to on-board over 100 premium ad campaigns by end of year, and over 100 local reps. If you have the skills to help us find premium advertisement opportunities on the east or west coast – why don't you join us for a coffee?

Ideally, business development reps help with lead generation, user on-boarding, proposals, purchase agreements, ad sponsors, and more. You'll attend tradeshows, private events, and be closely tied to our executive sales team.

We are a fully remote company, but have a studio office you can freely use in New York City, Toronto, and Los Angeles.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our product team to uncover actionable insights
  • Curate lists of leads and make contact
  • Update and maintain salesforce CRM database
  • Create proposals, purchase agreements, etc.
  • Examine cost estimates and project requirements

What We're Looking For

  • Fluent with Google Sheets and Docs
  • Helps if you know Adobe Creative (XD, Photoshop, Premiere), Figma, Slack, Final Cut Pro, Mac OS.
  • Ability to always deliver high-quality experiences
  • Obsessively independent and detail oriented
  • Super strong communicator
