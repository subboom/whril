Software Engineer, iOS

Fully Remote - North America

Hi I'm Chris and welcome to Whril! I started Whril because I've always enjoyed podcasts, music, and topical dicussions, but felt the experience has always been lack luster. So if you enjoy listening to people and want to help creators create, then read on!

Our Product

At Whril we're building a home for people who love to listen. Our goal is helping our creators connect with listeners at perfect moments in time. Currently, we are looking to hire an iOS Software Engineer, and our preference goes to someone who has experience with the Apple ecosystem, and loves to use the products they build.

Ideally, you will help us develop our iOS app, assist in api development, and develop from scratch new and exciting features for our audio platform. Naturally, Whril is a social tool, but knowledge of internet communication or social media is not required. We do like to hire people who use the products they build, are content creators, or actively participate in online communities. You will have room to architect, develop, test and deliver independently, or within a small team. We will work with you to further develop your skillsets and toolkit if interested. We want everyone to chase life goals, and take pride in supporting you.

We don't believe work occurs at a specific place or within set hours. We believe creativity and hard work can happen anywhere, anytime! We have studio offices that are avaliable for teams or individuals to use freely – our studio offices will open back up once we all feel safe.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our product team to uncover actionable insights
  • Architect, develop, test and deliver our iOS app
  • Assist in the development of our api, including our recommendation system
  • Build new features for creators and consumers
  • Help influence better product design

What We're Looking For

  • Fluent in Swift iOS, Golang, C or C++
  • Ability to deliver a high-quality experience
  • Highly independent and detail oriented
  • Strong design sense (UX/UI)
