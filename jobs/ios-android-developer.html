Software Developer, iOS+Android

North America

Overview

As a software developer, you will own our iOS and Android apps for Whril's mobile marketplace. You will oversee our api development and develop new features for Whril free and prime subscribers.

Knowledge of the online ad space is not necessary, but we like working with people who enjoy using the products they build. Ideally, you can architect, develop, test, and deliver on your own.

Qualifications

  • Fluent in Swift and Objective-C, Java and Kotlin
  • Ability to deliver frictionless experiences
  • Highly independent and detail oriented
  • Strong design sense (UX/UI)
