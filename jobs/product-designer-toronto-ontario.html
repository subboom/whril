Product Designer

Toronto, ON

Stadium is building a new generation of social platforms that promote instant discovery. If you like working with a high-impact, dynamic, and collaborative team that enjoys autonomy and empowerment...read on!

Stadium Product

At Stadium we want to inspire people to instantly discover what they love – with those they love. We have a fun time doing what we do. And enjoy creating new ways for people to discover online. If you ignore or adblock direct ads – than you should work with us.

We are expecting to on-board over 100 premium ad campaigns by end of year. If you have the skills to help us unlock premium advertisement opportunities on the west coast – why don't you join us for a coffee?

Product Managers help with the design and direction of product development. They play an essential role in UI/UX, customer relationships, and internal product execution.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our business development team to follow-up on actionable insights
  • Contribute to the overall product strategy and roadmap
  • Synthesize user research and qualitative feedback from a variety of sources to inform the product roadmap
  • Make micro-optimizations in all product experiences and flows to optimize for user love and product quality
  • Work with UX designers to improve the feel and vibe of the product via User Research and A/B testing
  • Deliver a fun user experience that rates highly in both user love and product finesse
  • Collaborate with development to ensuring our product is fast, fluid, and scalable at all times
  • Plan and lead requirements, project, and relevant competitive reviews with team and executive leadership
  • Track all defined deliverables from conception through launch, leveraging established processes, tools, and communications mechanisms within Stadium (Salesforce, Git, Slack, Mixpanel reports, etc)

What We're Looking For

  • Three or more years of program management experience with proven success in delivering end user facing products and services with preferably a UX background
  • Must have an eye for detail, polish, quality and passionate about User Love and Design
  • Must possess Product Management experience in Consumer or Social Products (ideally both)
  • The ability to wear many hats, learn quickly and change direction when needed
  • Proficiency in collecting, synthesizing and incorporating user-feedback and mapping it to the overall product vision and roadmap
  • The ability to think creatively and critically about product features, architecture, and capabilities
  • Innate curiosity and ability to self-start in an ambiguous and rapidly-changing startup environment
  • Strong project management, leadership, and cross-group collaboration skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication/presentation skills through all levels of the organization, technical and non-technical
  • Solid understanding of web and mobile technologies, service operations at scale, and ability to go deep in technical areas
  • Good problem-solving skills and ability to think strategically
  • Bachelor's degree or Masters in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience)
