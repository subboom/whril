Stadium Product

At Stadium we believe the world's best events deserve the world's biggest stadiums. We have a fun time doing what we do. And enjoy creating new ways for people to attend sport events online.

If you enjoy working in sports and have the skills to help us then why don't you join us for a coffee?

Our business development reps help with lead generation, customer on-boarding relationship management, proposals, purchase agreements, and more. You'll attend tradeshows, private events, and be closely tied to our executive sales team.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our product team to uncover actionable insights
  • Curate lists of leads and make contact
  • Update and maintain salesforce CRM database
  • Create proposals, purchase agreements, etc.
  • Examine cost estimates and project requirements

What We're Looking For

  • Fluent with Google Sheets and Docs
  • Helps if you know Adobe Creative (XD, Photoshop), Figma, Notion, Slack.
  • Ability to always deliver high-quality experiences
  • Obsessively independent and detail oriented
  • Super strong communicator
