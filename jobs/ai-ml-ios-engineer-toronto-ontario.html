Software Engineer, iOS AI/ML

Toronto, ON

Hi I'm Chris – I'm the founder of Stadium. If you like working with a high-impact, dynamic, and collaborative team that enjoys autonomy and empowerment...read on!

Stadium Product

At Stadium we want to inspire people to discover more of what they love – with those they love. We have a fun time doing what we do. And like making ads social and fun experiences. If you would like to kill the direct ad model, unwanted data-mining, and centralized networks controlling information flow online – than you should work with us.

You will own our webapp, our api, and build new features for our prime subscribers. Knowledge of the advertisement space is not required, but enthusiasm is appreciated. Ideally, you can architect, develop, test and deliver independently.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our product team to uncover actionable insights
  • Architect, code and test recommendation algorithms
  • Assist in the development of our recommendation system
  • Build new features for Stadium prime subscribers
  • Help influence better recommendation system design

What We're Looking For

  • Fluent in Go, iOS, TensorFlow
  • Ability to deliver high-quality recommendations
  • Highly independent and detail oriented
  • Strong design sense (UX/UI)
  • Great at developing ML algorithms
