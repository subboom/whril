Software Engineer, iOS

Toronto, ON

Hi i'm Chris, welcome to fien! If you enjoy working on ideas that get a lot of traction and you want to build fun products then read on!

Our Product

At fien we want to revolutionize texting because we believe texting is more than letters on a screen. Currently, we are looking to hire an iOS Software Engineer, our preference goes to someone who has experience or extreme enthusiasm for building high bandwidth text messaging platforms.

Ideally, you will own our iOS app, assist in api development, and build from scratch new and exciting features for our platform. Knowledge of internet communication or social media is not required, but we enjoy working with people who naturally care about the products they're building, and use the technology themselves. You will have room to architect, develop, test and deliver independently, or within a small team.

We are a remote-first company. We also have studio offices that are avaliable for teams or employees to use freely – our studio offices will open once the pandemic is over.

What You'll Do

  • Work with our product team to uncover actionable insights
  • Architect, develop, test and deliver our iOS app
  • Assist in the development of our api, including our recommendation system
  • Build new features for creators and consumers
  • Help influence better product design

What We're Looking For

  • Fluent in Swift iOS, Golang, C or C++ – functional and object oriented programming a must
  • Ability to deliver a high-quality experience
  • Highly independent and detail oriented
  • Strong design sense (UX/UI)
