Overview

Leading our creator partnerships, you will help us prospect, track and develop leads with content creators across a variety of social media platforms like TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, SnapChat, and more.

  • Helps if you know Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Slack, and/or Salesforce.
  • Ability to always deliver high-quality experiences
  • Obsessively independent and detail oriented
  • Super strong communicator
