Our Work

Whril is building a home that rewards people who advertise products they know, love, and trust.

Our platform connects people to brands, advertisers, sponsors, affiliates, companies, and products people actually want to share with their audience.

Our Hiring Process

When you apply, we will review your experience (past work, GitHub) and contributions (designs, products built, initiatives). We'll contact you to proceed.

The next step is a phone conversation where we get to know a bit more about you, and you about us.

Then we invite you in for a micro-intership where you get work next to our team on real problems with our product, strategy, or code base.

If you make it to our phone convo or internship, but no further, we'll let you know.
